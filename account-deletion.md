# Calvora account deletion

Effective date: April 27, 2026

## How to request deletion

Calvora users can request deletion of their account and associated app data by
email.

1. Send an email to `Algorise@usa.com`.
2. Use the subject line `Calvora account deletion request`.
3. Include the email address used to sign in to Calvora.
4. Wait for confirmation that the request was received.

## Data deleted

After verification, Calvora will delete account-linked data that is stored for
app functionality, where applicable. This may include:

- Firebase Authentication account data linked to Calvora.
- Cloud-synced calculation history.
- Cloud-synced settings, pinned tools, workspaces, and constants.
- Cloud sync metadata associated with the account.

## Data kept

Some data may be kept when required for security, legal compliance, abuse
prevention, or operational records. Crash reports and diagnostics may remain in
aggregated or de-identified form. Data stored only on the user's device must be
deleted by uninstalling the app or clearing app data on that device.

## Retention period

Deletion requests are reviewed as soon as reasonably possible. Some backup, log,
or diagnostic records may remain for up to 30 days before being removed from
active systems, unless a longer period is required by law or security needs.

## Contact

For questions about account deletion, contact: `Algorise@usa.com`
